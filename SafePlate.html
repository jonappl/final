SafePlate
Allergy-aware meal discovery

Find meals that fit real food needs.

SafePlate is a live recipe finder with filters for major allergens, extra ingredient avoids, diets, cuisines, meal types, cooking time, difficulty, and random meal ideas. Every recipe includes an image, ingredients, clear steps, and safety notes.

Safety note: This site is a planning tool, not medical advice. Always check ingredient labels, restaurant cross-contact risks, and medical guidance for severe allergies.

A flexible, colorful meal that can work for many allergy-aware diets.

Purpose

Help people quickly find meals that match allergy limits, diet choices, cooking style, and available time.

Audience

Students, families, home cooks, and anyone who needs organized allergy-aware meal ideas.

How it works

Pick foods to avoid, select a cuisine or diet, then open any recipe for ingredients and steps.

About SafePlate

Clear purpose, audience, and safety limits.

Clear purpose

SafePlate helps users filter meal ideas by allergies, ingredients, cuisine, food type, and cooking effort.

Defined audience

Designed for home cooks, students, families, and people who need organized meal ideas around allergy restrictions.

Responsible use

The app avoids recipes based on listed ingredient data, but it cannot verify packaged labels or cross-contact.
